Our Story

Tradition & Passion

Indy Punjabi Dhaba brings the authentic culinary experience of Punjab to Indianapolis. A "dhaba" is a traditional roadside restaurant in North India, where travelers stop to enjoy home-style dishes prepared with love and authentic spices.

Our mission is to serve traditional Punjabi dishes made with fresh ingredients and recipes passed down through generations. Every dish tells a story of tradition, family, and passion for Indian cuisine.
